A story telling night for Toronto tech workers.

Don't be grouped by the work you perform.

Life is about more than your startup, your app or what you do at your job.

Join us for an evening in a safe, supportive space.

Meet folks you haven't run into before, and listen to people share true, personal stories. Before attending, please read our code of conduct.

Code of Conduct

  • We want to be inclusive; do not engage in homophobic, racist, transphobic, ableist, sexist, or otherwise exclusionary behavior. Don’t make exclusionary jokes. Don't be a jerk.
  • Don’t harass people. Unconsented physical contact or sexual attention is harrassment. Consent is explicit; dressing or acting in a certain way is not consent.
  • Come and talk to us for any and all reasons.
  • Please be intentional in your actions and humble in your mistakes.
