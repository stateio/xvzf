Code of Conduct

We are dedicated to providing a safe environment for everyone in attendance, regardless of gender, orientation, ability, appearance, or text editor preference.

We will not tolerate harassment of any form, and we expect that all of our guests will do the same. We hope everyone will be aware of and work to resist the bystander effect in hurtful situations, and be ready to respond as appropriate.

In your stories, jokes, and conversations, be mindful of the fact that your words will be heard by many different people from many different backgrounds. In light of this, please be intentional in your language, and humble in your mistakes.

It is our goal that everyone will work to make this event enjoyable through intentional inclusivity, humility, and openness.
